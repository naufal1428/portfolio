LAB Tour & Travel Website

This project aims to develop a website for a travel agent named LAB Tour and Travel, which offers travel products and services to tourists by providing information on tour packages, complete details about the tourist attractions needed, recommended travel plans to various destinations, services for booking certified local tour guides at the destination, and a marketplace featuring local tourism products.

Role: UI/UX Designer

Duration: February 2024 - June 2024

  • Collaborated with project manager and developers to create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups.
  • Facilitated design sprints to rapidly ideate and prototype new features.
